# Article Studio

A place for creative articles. Creating, sharing and editing articles.

Made with Express.js, Next.js, MongoDB, and more...

See the project [here](https://github.com/aquapi/article-studio).

## Features

- Articles sorted by categories
- Searching articles
- Log in, sign up
- Modifying articles
- Vote for articles
- Add co-authors
- Public/private articles
